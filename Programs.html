Art Analysis Circle

Every Thursday, we gather to break down paintings, sculptures, and digital art through a critical lens. We explore themes, symbols, and contexts using art theory and historical knowledge.

Museum Study Trips

Monthly visits to museums and galleries—The Met, MoMA, The Whitney—where we engage in guided analysis and peer presentations on exhibitions. Our goal is to connect past and present trends.

Art History Dialogues

We hold discussion sessions around specific art movements, artists, and historical periods. Topics include Renaissance symbolism, postmodern critique, feminist art, and decolonial perspectives.

Guest Speakers & Panel Events

We invite professors, curators, and artists to share insights and research. Past guests include academic researchers, museum professionals, and emerging interdisciplinary creators.

Academic Mentoring

Club leaders and advisors offer support for students preparing research papers, presentations, or graduate art school portfolios. We help you go beyond participation—toward publication and academic recognition.
