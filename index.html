Our Mission

The Art Club exists for those who are passionate about the art world—its history, its present, and its future. Whether you're an artist, an art history student, or simply fascinated by how visual culture shapes society, this club offers a space to explore, reflect, and grow intellectually.

What We Offer

We host a variety of programs designed to deepen your understanding of art and its impact on culture. Our activities include:

Activity Description
Art Trends Weekly analysis and critique sessions on emerging art styles
Museum Trips Guided visits to galleries and museums across NYC
History Dialogues Group discussions on major movements and art theory
Peer Presentations Student-led breakdowns of genres like surrealism, Dada, etc.

From Caravaggio to Yayoi Kusama, we connect the dots between past and present, always asking: What is art trying to say, and why now?

Who Can Join?

This club is open to all majors! You don’t have to be an artist, just curious and ready to think deeply about visual culture. Our members include design majors, writers, history students, future curators, IT students, and more.
